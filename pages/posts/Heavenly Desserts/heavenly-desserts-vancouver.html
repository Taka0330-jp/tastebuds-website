Heavenly Desserts is Bringing a Slice of Sweet Luxury to Vancouver

Exciting news for dessert lovers! The UK’s premium dessert brand Heavenly Desserts is officially making its way to Vancouver’s West End. Founded in 2008 in the United Kingdom, Heavenly Desserts has become a favorite destination for indulgent, picture-perfect treats. With more than 60 locations worldwide, Heavenly Desserts is now bringing its signature sweetness to the Landmark on Robson.

You can expect a mix of brunch favorites and elegant desserts. Highlights include Biscoff and Banana French Toast, croffles, pistachio rose milk cakes, sticky toffee pudding, and decadent sundaes.

Pair your dessert with one of their handcrafted drinks or specialty lattes and enjoy the brand’s signature vibe of modern luxury and cozy indulgence.
