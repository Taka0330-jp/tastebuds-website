Heavenly Desserts is Bringing a Slice of Sweet Luxury to Vancouver

Bryan Vo - October 19, 2025

1. Quick Intro

Heavenly Desserts is an international dessert chain that started in the U.K. back in 2008, famous for its luxury sweets — think croffles (a mix of croissant + waffle), sundaes, gelato, milkshakes, and so on. In Canada, they opened their first store in Mississauga, Ontario, as part of their North American expansion. However — there isn’t yet an official location in Vancouver, B.C., according to their official site

2. What Makes Heavenly Desserts Special


Visually stunning desserts: Every dish looks like it’s auditioning for the front page of Pinterest. Glossy sauces, perfect plating, pastel tones — aesthetic perfection.
Perfect match for creative souls: If you’re a designer, developer, or digital artist, this place is practically a mood board in physical form. Their use of light, color, and shape feels like UI design you can eat.
Old & new fusion: Croffle says it all — old-world pastry meets new-school experimentation. It’s classic flavor with a modern interface.
Dream scenario for Vancouver: Imagine one opening on Main Street or Yaletown: soft jazz playing, rainy streets outside, people sipping hot chocolate while editing photos — straight vibes.

3. Final Thoughts — The Sweet Side of Imagination

Heavenly Desserts may not have a Vancouver address yet, but it’s already a vibe the city deserves — elegant, comforting, and creativity

It reminds me that design isn’t just on screens — it’s in the way a plate is styled, the way light hits a spoon, the way a story tastes when told through flavor.

For local creatives craving a space that blends art, food, and atmosphere, Heavenly Desserts feels like the perfect fit. Until their grand opening here, I’ll keep imagining the smell of warm croffles on Granville Street — and maybe, design the digital version first.
