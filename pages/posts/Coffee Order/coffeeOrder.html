What Your Coffee Order Says About You – Vancouver Edition

Esme Chan - Oct 04 , 2025 - Food Fun Facts

In a city that runs on caffeine and rain, coffee is basically Vancouver’s love language. Whether you’re working from a cozy café in Mount Pleasant, grabbing a to-go cup before a Stanley Park walk, or sipping something iced on Commercial Drive in mid-January, your order says a lot about you. Here’s what your go-to Vancouver coffee might be revealing.

1. Americano

You are a purist. You appreciate simplicity, strength, and a good roast. You probably have a favorite local café that knows your name and your dog’s name. You work remotely, read The Tyee, and own a reusable cup that’s seen more rain than sunshine

2. Iced Oat Milk Latte

You’re the definition of effortlessly put-together. It’s raining, it’s 8°C, and you’re still ordering it iced because “it just tastes better.” You own multiple Stanley cups, have a tote full of notebooks, and your go-to Starbucks is whichever one has the best lighting for a story post. You say you drink it for the caffeine, but deep down, it’s for the aesthetic.

3. Cappuccino

You’re all about cozy energy. You like your coffee with a bit of foam and a lot of intention. You believe in soft mornings, rainy playlists, and people-watching at indie cafés on Main Street. If someone’s bringing you coffee, you’ll remind them “extra foam, please.”

4. Double Double

You’re classic, no-fuss, and proudly loyal to your Canadian roots.. You probably grew up on Timmies runs and still think it hits the spot. You’re the friend who keeps everyone grounded and always shows up first for brunch. You might have upgraded to artisan doughnuts, but your heart still belongs to the drive-thru.

5. Flat White

You’ve got a refined taste. You know your espresso from your ristretto and aren’t afraid to tell people that. You lean toward the artsy side, film cameras, local galleries, and minimal interiors speak to your soul. You spend weekends wandering through Main Street galleries or reading in hidden cafés that don’t show up on Google Maps..

6. Cold Brew

You’re all about efficiency and caffeine that hits. You bike to work, own way too many Arc’teryx jackets. You plan hikes before noon and think “slow mornings” are for other people. You like your coffee strong and your playlists stronger.

7. Matcha Latte (with oat or almond milk)

You give wellness influencer energy but in a grounded way. You believe in morning rituals, crystal shops, and journaling on rainy afternoons. You love the green tea aesthetic as much as the “earthy flavor” . You are probably the calm friend who wears Aritzia, talks about “energy” without irony and still reminds everyone to hydrate.

8. Pumpkin Spice Latte (Seasonal Queen)

You’ve been waiting for this since August. You love fall candles, oversized scarves, and pretending it’s sweater weather even when it’s still 18°C. You know every café with the best pumpkin spice mix in the city and can spot good latte art from across the room. You’ll take your PSL to go while planning your next photo spot at Stanley Park. You’re not basic, you’re festive.
