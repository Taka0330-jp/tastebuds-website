Enjoy a Taiwanese-style chicken burger at Zhengxin Chicken Steak

Taka Kondo - October 19, 2025

Walking near Metrotown Mall, a bright red exterior with a large crown chicken character catches your eye. This is the popular Taiwanese chain Zhengxin Chicken Steak (正新鸡排). A brand familiar in mainland China and Taiwan has finally made its way to Canada!

A hearty chicken burger


The crispy fried chicken, fresh lettuce, and fluffy sweet bun make for an amazing combination. The chicken coating is especially thick, giving it a satisfying crunch. With every bite, juicy meat juices spread throughout. It's more substantial than it looks, and one burger is a satisfyingly filling size.

Free Monk Fruit and Chrysanthemum Tea


Inside the store, self-service “Monk Fruit and Chrysanthemum Tea” was available. I tried it for the first time this visit, a subtly sweet, refreshing drink perfect after fried food. Its gentle flavor evokes memories of Taiwanese street stalls.

The atmosphere inside the restaurant


The interior has a bright, pop atmosphere and is filled with a liveliness reminiscent of Taiwanese street food culture. The red and yellow interior is accented by a glowing neon sign featuring a chicken character, creating a fun, theme park-like atmosphere. The ceiling lighting is bright, and the menu is clearly displayed above the counter. Green chairs and white tables create a clean yet welcoming atmosphere. It was a bright and friendly space where you could easily stop by, whether alone or with a group.

Zhengxin recreates authentic Taiwanese street food. Crispy chicken burgers paired with subtly sweet chrysanthemum tea. For anyone in Vancouver craving that “Taiwan night market vibe,” this is a spot I highly recommend.
